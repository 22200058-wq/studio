# **App Name**: SariStore Insight

## Core Features:

- Inventory Management: Allows users to add, edit, delete, and view product details including name, category, buy price, sell price, and stock quantity.
- Point of Sale (POS) System: A user-friendly interface for processing sales by selecting items, entering quantities, calculating totals, and recording transactions. Automatically updates product stock.
- Transaction History View: Enables users to browse and review past sales, displaying details for each transaction, including products sold and their quantities.
- Profit & Sales Dashboard: Displays key business metrics such as daily, weekly, or monthly profit and total sales volume, derived from recorded transactions and product cost data.
- AI Sales Predictor & Advisor: A tool that analyzes historical sales patterns and current stock levels to predict demand for popular items, suggest optimal reorder points, and generate basic marketing advice for inventory optimization.
- Cloud Database Integration: Integrates with a robust cloud-based relational database to securely store and efficiently retrieve all product, sales, and transaction data, replacing the local SQLite file recommended in the original prompt for web-based scalability.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A calm and muted green (#5E8F66) to convey growth and reliability, fitting a practical business application.
- Background color: A very light, desaturated green (#F5FAF6) providing a clean, open, and inviting aesthetic.
- Accent color: A lively chartreuse (#C7E05E) that offers strong contrast for highlighting interactive elements and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a clean and modern sans-serif typeface, ensuring excellent readability for all data and text within the application.
- Utilize a consistent set of minimalist line-art style icons for all functionalities, providing clear and easily recognizable visual cues for inventory, sales, and analytics features.
- Employ a clean, modular, and card-based layout structure with intuitive navigation to clearly separate sections like Inventory, POS, and Dashboard, ensuring a user-friendly and organized interface.
- Incorporate subtle hover effects on buttons and interactive elements, along with smooth, understated transitions when switching between different views or updating content, to enhance user experience without distraction.
